What is Altus?

Altus Disputatio, the flagship event of NUALS, follows a patented format of Debate called The Devilâ€™s Cross. The contest begins with a draw of lots to determine the proponents and opponents. The competitors face off against each other on a given phrase by interpreting it and justifying their arguments. Based on the reasoning posited, either one of the interpretations is chosen, and the floor is set for debate. Post the debate, the contestants shall face questions from the panel, including an inquisitor.
